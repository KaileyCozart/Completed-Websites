Theater


My Acting Experience:
Aside from a few skits, I had never performed in a play. During junior year, I had the opportunity of playing a slightly deranged, and very unpleasant, old lady. It was great to learn the fundamentals of acting and form stronger relationships with the other actors. In March of 2016, I played Madame Pernelle in Moliere's "Tartuffe."
